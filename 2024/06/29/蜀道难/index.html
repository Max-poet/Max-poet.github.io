The Endless Mountain

The pattering rain softly whispered, as if sharing secrets with the green moss under the bamboo pavilion. Hearing the flood’s roar broke through the silence, villagers urged me to hurry on my way. The peaks and ridges seemed poised to split, as the sweep waves were collapsing. But what I behold was the roots of the yellow camphor tree entwining around the stones, with the yellow camphor orchid blooming as well.

蜀道难

雨落竹亭青苔生
忽闻山蛟催客行
峰峦欲裂飞檐坠
桷根盘石兰未尽

淅沥的小雨打落在了竹亭上，青苔远离世俗的目光，悄悄来到无人知晓的地方，慢慢地生长着。我忽然听到洪水呼啸而来的声音，村名催着我赶路不要停下；山峰和山峦像是要裂开了一样，房屋的飞檐坠落，但我却看见了黄桷树的根盘着石头不动，而黄桷兰依旧盛开
